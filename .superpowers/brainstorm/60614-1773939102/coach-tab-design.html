COACH STATUS HERO

Tier 2 elevated card at top. Emerald breathing glow = agent presence. Shows today's scheduled workout and training load status. Replaces the generic "What's on the agenda?" with actionable context.

SECTION LABELS

Whoop-style uppercase micro labels with tracking. Creates strong visual hierarchy and makes sections scannable. Used consistently across all screens.

QUICK ACTIONS

Grouped list instead of 2x2 grid. Top action is contextual ("Start today's session" with emerald START label — earned color). Others recede to neutral chevrons. More scannable, less visual noise.

RECENT CONVERSATIONS

Tier 0 flat rows — no cards, no borders. Content flows on bg. Reduces card fatigue and creates visual breathing room after the hero and actions.
